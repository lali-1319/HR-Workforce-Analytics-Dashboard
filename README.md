 📊 HR Workforce Analytics Dashboard
Attrition, Satisfaction & Employee Insights

---

 📌 Project Overview

This project presents an **interactive HR Analytics Dashboard** built using **Power BI** to analyze employee data and uncover key insights related to **attrition, job satisfaction, and workforce behavior**.

The dashboard helps organizations understand:

* Why employees leave
* Which departments are most affected
* How satisfaction and demographics influence attrition

---
 🎯 Objectives

* Analyze employee attrition patterns
* Identify key factors influencing employee turnover
* Provide actionable insights for HR decision-making
* Build an interactive and visually appealing dashboard

---

 🛠️ Tools & Technologies

* **Power BI** – Dashboard creation & visualization
* **Python (Jupyter Notebook)** – Data cleaning & preprocessing
* **Pandas, Matplotlib, Seaborn** – Data analysis & EDA

---

📂 Dataset Description

The dataset contains **employee-level information** including:

* Employee ID (`emp_no`)
* Age & Age Band
* Gender
* Department & Job Role
* Education & Education Field
* Business Travel
* Marital Status
* Job Satisfaction
* Attrition Status
* Active Employee Status

---

📊 Dashboard Features

🔹 Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

* Total Employees
* Attrition Count
* Attrition Rate (%)
* Active Employees

---

🔹 Visual Insights

  Attrition by Department (Treemap)
  Attrition Trend by Age Band (Line Chart)
  Attrition by Job Role (Bar Chart)
  Business Travel vs Attrition (Column Chart)
  verage Job Satisfaction by Department


🔹 Interactive Filters (Slicers)

* Department
* Job Role
* Gender
* Age Band


🔍 Key Insights

* Employees with **low job satisfaction** show higher attrition
* Certain departments (e.g., R&D, Sales) have higher workforce concentration
* **Frequent business travel** is associated with increased attrition
* Younger employees tend to switch jobs more frequently
* Workforce distribution varies significantly across job roles

 💡 Business Impact

This dashboard enables HR teams to:

* Improve employee retention strategies
* Identify high-risk departments
* Enhance employee satisfaction programs
* Make data-driven workforce decisions
